What You Get — Scope of Work

This is a fully custom online store — not a template. It has two sides: the customer website and a private admin panel to run the whole business, plus online payments and WhatsApp alerts built in.

🛍️ Customer Website ✓ Complete

  • Modern, mobile-friendly storefront
  • Product browsing, search & categories
  • Product detail pages with photo galleries
  • Shopping cart & slide-out cart drawer
  • Wishlist / favourites per customer account
  • Customer accounts, login & order history
  • Checkout & order tracking
  • Reviews, product Q&A & sale pages

📊 Admin Panel ✓ Complete

  • Dashboard with sales charts & insights
  • Product management with real photo uploads
  • Bulk product import (file upload)
  • Order management & status updates
  • Customer list & details
  • Promotions, discounts & coupon codes
  • Sales reports & staff accounts
  • Store settings & secure admin login

Included Integrations

💳 Online Payment Gateway — SSLCommerz In Development

Customers pay online using cards, bKash, Nagad, Rocket and internet banking — all through a single, trusted Bangladeshi gateway. Money settles directly to the business bank account. There is no monthly fee; the gateway charges a small percentage per successful sale only.

📱 WhatsApp Business Automation In Development

The website automatically sends the admin WhatsApp messages, so the shop can be managed from the phone without logging in. Included alerts:

  • New order alert — instantly when a customer places an order
  • Low-stock alert — when a product is about to run out
  • Back-in-stock update — when a product is restocked
  • Weekly sales summary — total orders & revenue every week
  • Monthly sales report — a bigger-picture view each month

Set up on the official WhatsApp Business platform. For admin-only alerts the running cost stays within the free monthly allowance in normal use.
